About Us

FF Solution is a professional recruiting company working with 200+ trusted carriers across the USA. We place Company Drivers and Owner Operators on Dry Van & Reefer freight with strong miles and reliable pay.

Minimum requirement: 1+ year OTR experience.

Pay & Packages

1–2 years experience

  • Pay: 25% of gross or up to 60 CPM
  • Daily miles: 800+ (approved)
  • Weekly miles: 4000 (Mon–Fri)
  • Working weeks: minimum 3 weeks ON
  • Home time: 3 weeks ON → 3 days OFF; 4 weeks ON → 4–5 days OFF (max 5)

2+ years experience

  • Pay: 27% of gross or up to 65 CPM
  • Other terms same as above

Hazmat / Tanker / Flatbed

Hazmat / Tanker / Flatbed loads pay higher. Driver can choose 70 CPM or 30% for those loads.

Detention (waiting/layover) is paid by carriers. No unload pay.

Position Types & Rules

  • Solo: Male drivers only
  • Team: Male + Male only
  • Female drivers (solo or team) are not accepted
  • Mixed male–female teams are not accepted
  • If a male driver needs a teammate, we can help find one
  • No forced dispatch — driver approves loads
